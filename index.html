About the Ontario Highland Games

Since 1948, the Glengarry Highland Games have set the standard. Our Ontario festival carries that torch ‚Äî celebrating Scottish culture, athletics, music, and community.

We bring together thousands of visitors each year for a weekend of pageantry: massed bands, heavyweight competitions, highland dancing, fiddling, and family fun. The Games are organized by the Kenyon Agricultural Society, a volunteer-driven nonprofit dedicated to preserving Scottish heritage in the heart of Ontario.
